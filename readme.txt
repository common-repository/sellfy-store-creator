Sellfy - simplest way to sell digital downloads. Sellfy Store allows to add Sellfy seller's profile page to your WordPress blog.

== Description ==

[Sellfy](https://sellfy.com/ "Sellfy - Sell digital downloads") is an e-commerce platform for selling digital downloads directly to your audience. Upload and sell ebooks, videos, music, comics, designs or anything else digital you create. [Sellfy](https://sellfy.com/ "Sellfy - Sell digital downloads") will hist your products and automatically deliver them to customers after the purchase.

= Features =
* Connect with most popular payment methods - PayPal, Stripe and Paymill;
* We do not limit total storage, bandwidth and you can have unlimited number of products
* Use powerful marketing tools to increase your sales

If you don't have Sellfy account, create one [here](https://sellfy.com/signup/ "Register to Sellfy - Sell digital downloads")

== Installation ==
* Upload wp-sellfy.zip through Plugins->Add New, or extract and upload the folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
* Activate the plugin through the \'Plugins\' menu in WordPress
